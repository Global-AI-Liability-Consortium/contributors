# Contributors

The Global AI Liability Consortium advances AI liability through the collective contributions of researchers, practitioners, professionals, institutions, policymakers, educators, and subject matter experts.

The Contributors Program recognizes that enduring knowledge is built through collaboration across disciplines, professions, industries, and jurisdictions.

Together, contributors strengthen the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

> **Liability is where AI disciplines converge.**

---

# Purpose

The Contributors Program exists to provide meaningful opportunities for individuals and institutions to contribute knowledge, expertise, experience, and professional insight to the Global AI Liability Consortium.

Contributors support GAILC's mission by strengthening its Knowledge Hubs, Research Program, Publications, Standards Program, and Institutional Partnership Program.

---

# Contributor Philosophy

The advancement of AI liability requires expertise from many disciplines.

No single profession, organization, or jurisdiction possesses all the knowledge required to govern AI responsibly.

GAILC welcomes contributions that strengthen institutional understanding, promote professional excellence, and advance responsible AI accountability.
